Family of Nightingales

Nightingale Inn was first founded in 1787 by Walter Knight, Freemason from London, UK. He opened the first Inn with his wife, Marilyn Knight, in downtown London before the steampunk age gripped Europe. He drove a lot of business from travelers and tourist of the time. He was involved in various trade deals that helped traders of the UK stay in a comforting Inn while in town. Ever since the family business has been passed down from generation to generation with the trusted famly run business thriving ever since. Using exeptional customer service in catering to each and every need of their fellow guests.
